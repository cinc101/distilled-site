Why Distilled?

Your to-do list is endless. Your time isn't. Distilled intelligently selects the 3 most important tasks for you to focus on today.

  • Smart Scoring: Prioritizes based on due dates, importance, and smart keywords.
  • Zero Setup: Works directly with your existing Apple Reminders.
  • Widget & Live Activity: Keep your focus visible right on your home screen.
  • Habit Building: Track your daily consistency with streaks and heatmaps.
